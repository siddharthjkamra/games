What?

J2ME (Java 2 Micro Edition) games are designed for mobile phones with limited hardware and software capabilities, primarily used on older mobile phones.

Where?

J2ME games were popular on a variety of devices, especially in the early 2000s, before the rise of smartphones.

How?

You can download and play J2ME games on compatible mobile devices using emulators or specific mobile apps that support this format.
